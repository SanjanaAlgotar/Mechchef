Cook FAQs

Do I need a food hygiene certificate?+
Yes — all MechChef cooks must hold a minimum Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate. If you don't have one, we'll point you to a free online course you can complete in a day. It's a legal requirement for selling food in the UK.
How much can I earn?+
Earnings vary by how many days you cook and your menu prices. Our cooks typically earn between £150–£600 per month cooking 2–5 days a week. You keep the full meal price minus a small platform fee (shown during onboarding).
Do I handle my own deliveries?+
No — MechChef handles all deliveries. You simply cook and have the food ready by the agreed time. Our courier will collect and deliver to the customer. You never need to leave your kitchen.
Can I cook multiple cuisines?+
Absolutely. You can list dishes from multiple cuisines on your profile. Many of our cooks offer a mix — for example, Indian mains with Mediterranean salads. You decide what's on your menu.
What about ingredients and packaging?+
You are responsible for sourcing your own ingredients. MechChef provides eco-friendly packaging at cost price, or you can source your own. We'll share packaging guidelines during onboarding.
Is there a minimum number of orders I have to take?+
No minimum. You set your availability and can pause or stop cooking at any time with 24 hours notice. There's no penalty for taking breaks — cook when it works for you.
When and how do I get paid?+
Earnings from the previous week are transferred to your bank account every Monday morning. You'll receive a full breakdown of orders and earnings in your dashboard.
