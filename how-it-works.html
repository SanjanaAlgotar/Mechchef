Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh is the food?+
All meals are cooked fresh on the morning of your delivery. Cooks never prepare food in advance — every dish is made to order specifically for you.
How far in advance do I need to order?+
Typically 1–2 days in advance so your cook has time to shop for ingredients and prepare your meal. Some cooks allow same-day orders — this is shown on their profile.
Is the food safe? Are cooks verified?+
Yes. Every MechChef cook holds a minimum Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate (FSA-approved) and passes our kitchen safety video walkthrough before going live. All cooks are re-verified annually.
What areas do you deliver to?+
We are currently launching in Harrow and Newham, London. More areas coming soon — join the waitlist to be notified when we reach your area.
What is the delivery fee?+
A flat £1.99 delivery fee applies to all orders, regardless of order size. There is no minimum order value.
Can I cancel or get a refund?+
You can cancel up to 12 hours before your delivery slot for a full refund. After that, cancellations are not accepted as the cook will have already started preparing your meal.
How do I become a home cook on MechChef?+
Visit our Cook with us page to register your interest. We'll be in touch to verify your food hygiene certificate and complete onboarding.
