1 Our Commitment to Food Safety

MechChef is committed to ensuring the food prepared and delivered through our platform is safe for consumption. We require all cooks on our platform to meet UK food hygiene standards before going live.

✅ All MechChef cooks must hold a valid Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate from an accredited UK provider (e.g. Highfield, RSPH, CIEH) before their first order.

2 UK Legal Compliance

  • Food Safety Act 1990 — general food safety requirements
  • Food Hygiene Regulations 2006 — hygiene standards for food businesses
  • UK Food Information Regulations 2014 — allergen labelling requirements
  • Natasha's Law (PPDS Regulations, October 2021) — full ingredient and allergen labelling
  • Food Standards Agency (FSA) guidance — food safety management procedures

⚠️ Cooks who prepare food for sale in the UK may be required to register as a food business with their local authority.

3 The 14 Major Allergens

Under UK law, the following 14 allergens must be declared when present as an ingredient:

🌾Cereals/Gluten
🦐Crustaceans
🥚Eggs
🐟Fish
🥜Peanuts
🫘Soybeans
🥛Milk
🌰Nuts
🌿Celery
🟡Mustard
🌱Sesame
🍷Sulphites
🐌Lupin
🦑Molluscs

⚠️ If you have a severe food allergy, always check the allergen information on each meal listing AND contact the cook directly before ordering.

4 Natasha's Law

Natasha's Law (October 2021) requires full ingredient and allergen labelling on all pre-packed for direct sale food. For MechChef, every meal listing must display a full ingredient list and highlight allergens in bold.

5 Food Hygiene Standards for Cooks

  • Wash hands thoroughly before and during food preparation
  • Use separate chopping boards for raw meat, vegetables, and cooked food
  • Store food at correct temperatures (below 5°C for chilled, above 63°C for hot food)
  • Do not cook when ill with vomiting, diarrhoea, or infectious skin conditions
  • Keep kitchen clean and free from pests
  • Use food-safe packaging for delivery
  • Label all packaged meals with meal name, ingredients, and allergens

6 Reporting Food Safety Concerns

  • Email: safety@mechchef.co.uk
  • In the case of a medical emergency, call 999 immediately.
  • Food safety concerns can also be reported to the Food Standards Agency at food.gov.uk.

1 Getting Started

Welcome to MechChef! We connect passionate home cooks with customers who crave real, home-cooked food. Here's how to get set up:

  1. Create your cook account — Register at mechchef.vercel.app and select "I'm a Cook" during signup.
  2. Complete your profile — Add your name, cuisine type, a short bio, and a profile photo.
  3. Upload your Food Hygiene Certificate — A valid Level 2 certificate is mandatory.
  4. Add your meals — List your dishes with photos, descriptions, allergens, and pricing (£5–£10).
  5. Set your availability — Tell customers which days and time slots you cook.
  6. Wait for approval — Our admin team reviews all new cook accounts within 48 hours.

✅ Once approved, you're live! Customers in your area can find and order from you immediately.

2 Meal Listings

  • Real photo of your actual food — This is the most important thing.
  • Clear, accurate description — Ingredients, cooking method, spice level, serving size.
  • All allergens declared — Required by UK law. Do not skip this.
  • Honest pricing — Meals must be priced between £5.00 and £10.00.

⚠️ Meal photos must be original photos of food you have actually prepared. Stock images are not allowed and will result in meal removal.

3 Managing Orders

  • Accept or reject within 30 minutes — If you don't respond, the order is automatically cancelled.
  • Update order status — Mark as Cooking → Ready → Delivered as you progress.
  • Never cancel without good reason — Repeated cancellations affect your rating and may lead to account suspension.

✅ Do

  • Accept orders you can definitely fulfil
  • Update status in real time
  • Package food safely and hygienically
  • Label every package with meal name and allergens

❌ Don't

  • Accept orders when you're unavailable
  • Ignore order notifications
  • Cancel last-minute without contacting us
  • Ask customers to pay outside the platform

4 Earnings & Payouts

  • MechChef charges a platform commission on each completed order.
  • Payouts are processed weekly to your registered bank account.
  • You are an independent contractor, not an employee of MechChef.
  • Tax responsibility: Any income earned through MechChef must be declared to HMRC.

⚠️ If your total self-employment income exceeds £1,000 per tax year, you must register as self-employed and complete a Self Assessment tax return.

5 Food Business Registration

Under UK law, anyone running a food business — including home cooks selling food regularly — may need to register with their local authority. Registration is free and must be done at least 28 days before starting to trade.

  • Find your local authority at: food.gov.uk

⚠️ Failure to register when required is a criminal offence.

6 Account Suspension & Removal

MechChef may suspend or remove a cook account for repeated cancellations, negative feedback, failure to declare allergens, poor food hygiene, or any violation of our Terms & Conditions.

Cook support: cooks@mechchef.co.uk
