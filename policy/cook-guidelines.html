For Cooks

Cook Guidelines

Everything you need to know to cook and earn on MechChef. Welcome to the community!

1 Getting Started

Welcome to MechChef! We connect passionate home cooks with customers who crave real, home-cooked food. Here's how to get set up:

  1. Create your cook account — Register at mechchef.vercel.app and select "I'm a Cook" during signup.
  2. Complete your profile — Add your name, cuisine type, a short bio, and a profile photo. Customers choose cooks they trust.
  3. Upload your Food Hygiene Certificate — A valid Level 2 certificate is mandatory. Our team will verify it before approving your account.
  4. Add your meals — List your dishes with photos, descriptions, allergens, and pricing (£5–£10).
  5. Set your availability — Tell customers which days and time slots you cook. Only appear online when you're ready.
  6. Wait for approval — Our admin team reviews all new cook accounts within 48 hours.

✅ Once approved, you're live! Customers in your area can find and order from you immediately.

2 Meal Listings

What makes a great listing

  • Real photo of your actual food — This is the most important thing. Customers eat with their eyes first.
  • Clear, accurate description — Ingredients, cooking method, spice level, serving size.
  • All allergens declared — Required by UK law. Do not skip this.
  • Honest pricing — Meals must be priced between £5.00 and £10.00.

Pricing guidance

MechChef is a tiffin-style platform — affordable, everyday home cooking. We recommend:

  • Simple dal/sabzi combos: £5.00 – £6.00
  • Biryani, curry with rice/roti: £6.00 – £8.00
  • Special meals, larger portions: £8.00 – £10.00

⚠️ Meal photos must be original photos of food you have actually prepared. Stock images or photos from the internet are not allowed and will result in meal removal.

3 Managing Orders

When a customer places an order with you, you will receive a notification on your dashboard. You must:

  • Accept or reject within 30 minutes — If you don't respond, the order is automatically cancelled and the customer refunded.
  • Update order status — Mark as Cooking → Ready → Delivered as you progress.
  • Never cancel without good reason — Repeated cancellations affect your rating and may lead to account suspension.

✅ Do

  • Accept orders you can definitely fulfil
  • Update status in real time
  • Contact MechChef if there's an emergency
  • Package food safely and hygienically
  • Label every package with meal name and allergens

❌ Don't

  • Accept orders when you're unavailable
  • Ignore order notifications
  • Cancel last-minute without contacting us
  • Send food different from your listing
  • Ask customers to pay outside the platform

4 Availability & Schedule

  • Set your available days and delivery time slots in your cook dashboard.
  • Only switch to "Live" when you are genuinely ready to cook and receive orders.
  • If you need a day off, turn off availability in advance — don't just ignore orders.
  • We understand life happens — maternity leave, holidays, illness. Just keep your schedule updated so customers aren't disappointed.

✅ MechChef is designed for flexible cooking. Whether you cook 2 days a week or 5, you are in control of your schedule.

5 Earnings & Payouts

  • MechChef charges a platform commission on each completed order. Your net earnings are shown in your dashboard analytics.
  • Payouts are processed weekly to your registered bank account.
  • You are an independent contractor, not an employee of MechChef.
  • Tax responsibility: Any income earned through MechChef must be declared to HMRC. If your total self-employment income exceeds £1,000 per tax year, you must register as self-employed and complete a Self Assessment tax return.

⚠️ We strongly recommend keeping records of your MechChef earnings. For tax advice, consult HMRC's self-employment guidance at gov.uk or speak to an accountant.

6 Food Business Registration

Under UK law, anyone running a food business — including home cooks selling food regularly — may need to register with their local authority as a food business operator. This applies if you are selling food regularly (not just occasionally).

  • Registration is free and must be done at least 28 days before starting to trade.
  • Contact your local council's Environmental Health department to register.
  • Find your local authority at: food.gov.uk

⚠️ MechChef requires all cooks who fulfil regular orders to confirm they have registered (or confirmed they are exempt) with their local authority. Failure to register when required is a criminal offence.

7 Account Suspension & Removal

MechChef may suspend or remove a cook account for:

  • Repeated order cancellations without valid reason
  • Repeated negative customer feedback or complaints
  • Failure to declare allergens accurately
  • Evidence of poor food hygiene standards
  • Attempting to take payments outside the platform
  • Any violation of our Terms & Conditions

We will always try to resolve issues before taking action. If you receive a warning, please contact us at cooks@mechchef.co.uk.
