Transparency

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to keep our platform running and to understand how people use it. Here's exactly what we use and why.

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and work properly. Most websites use them.

✅ We use only essential cookies by default. Analytics cookies are only placed with your consent.

2 Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
sb-access-token Essential Keeps you logged in to your MechChef account (Supabase Auth) Session / 1 hour
sb-refresh-token Essential Refreshes your login session automatically 30 days
mechchef-location Essential Remembers your selected delivery area (Harrow / Newham) 30 days
mechchef-cart Essential Stores your basket items while you browse Session
_vercel_analytics Analytics Anonymised page view analytics via Vercel (no personal data) 90 days

3 Essential vs Analytics Cookies

Essential Cookies

These are strictly necessary for MechChef to function. Without them you cannot log in, place orders, or use the platform. Under UK GDPR, we do not need your consent to set these cookies as they are required for the service you have requested.

Analytics Cookies

We use Vercel Analytics to understand how people use MechChef — which pages are most visited, where users drop off, and how to improve the experience. This data is anonymised and we cannot identify individual users from it.

You may opt out of analytics cookies at any time by contacting us or via your browser settings.

4 Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here's how for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using MechChef properly.
