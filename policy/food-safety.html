Food Safety & Allergen Policy

Your safety is our priority. All meals on MechChef are prepared by verified home cooks following UK food safety standards.

1 Our Commitment to Food Safety

MechChef is committed to ensuring the food prepared and delivered through our platform is safe for consumption. We require all cooks on our platform to meet UK food hygiene standards before going live.

✅ All MechChef cooks must hold a valid Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate from an accredited UK provider (e.g. Highfield, RSPH, CIEH) before their first order.

2 UK Legal Compliance

MechChef and our cooks operate in compliance with the following UK legislation:

  • Food Safety Act 1990 — general food safety requirements
  • Food Hygiene Regulations 2006 — hygiene standards for food businesses
  • UK Food Information Regulations 2014 — allergen labelling requirements
  • Natasha's Law (PPDS Regulations, October 2021) — full ingredient and allergen labelling on pre-packed for direct sale food
  • Food Standards Agency (FSA) guidance — food safety management procedures

⚠️ Cooks who prepare food for sale in the UK may be required to register as a food business with their local authority. We advise all cooks to check their registration obligations with their local council.

⚠️ If you have a severe food allergy, always check the allergen information on each meal listing AND contact the cook directly before ordering. MechChef cannot guarantee meals are free from cross-contamination in home kitchen environments.

4 Natasha's Law — What It Means for MechChef

Natasha's Law (October 2021) requires full ingredient and allergen labelling on all pre-packed for direct sale (PPDS) food — that is, food packaged at the same place it is sold.

For MechChef, this means:

  • Every meal listing must display a full ingredient list and highlight allergens in bold
  • Cooks must label packaged meals with the meal name and all ingredients before delivery
  • Allergens must be emphasised (bold, underlined, or different colour) within the ingredient list

MechChef's platform captures allergen information from cooks at the meal creation stage and displays it clearly to customers on every meal listing.

5 Food Hygiene Standards for Cooks

All cooks on MechChef must follow these minimum hygiene standards:

  • Wash hands thoroughly before and during food preparation
  • Use separate chopping boards for raw meat, vegetables, and cooked food
  • Store food at correct temperatures (below 5°C for chilled, above 63°C for hot food)
  • Do not cook when ill with vomiting, diarrhoea, or infectious skin conditions
  • Keep kitchen clean and free from pests
  • Use food-safe packaging for delivery
  • Label all packaged meals with meal name, ingredients, and allergens

6 Reporting Food Safety Concerns

If you experience a food safety issue — such as foreign objects in food, suspected food poisoning, or mislabelled allergens — please contact us immediately:

  • Email: safety@mechchef.co.uk
  • In the case of a medical emergency, call 999 immediately.
  • Food safety concerns can also be reported to your local council's Environmental Health department or the Food Standards Agency at food.gov.uk.
