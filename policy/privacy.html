1 Who We Are

MechChef is the data controller responsible for your personal data. We operate the MechChef food delivery platform connecting home cooks with customers in London.

Contact: privacy@mechchef.co.uk

✅ MechChef is committed to processing your data lawfully, fairly, and transparently in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2 What Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery address(es)
  • Role (customer or cook)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Order Information

  • Meals ordered, quantities, and special requests
  • Delivery address and slot preferences
  • Payment method type (we do not store card numbers — these are handled by Stripe)
  • Order status and history

Cook-Specific Data

  • Food Hygiene Certificate details
  • Kitchen address and availability schedule
  • Meal listings, photos, and pricing
  • Earnings and payout information

Technical Data

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Cookies and usage data (see Section 8)

3 How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To send order confirmations and status updates
  • To verify cook credentials and manage approvals
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g. food safety regulations)
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent, which you can withdraw at any time)

⚠️ We will never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4 Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Contract performance — to provide our service, process orders, and manage your account
  • Legal obligation — to comply with food safety laws, financial regulations, and HMRC requirements
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our platform, prevent fraud, and ensure security
  • Consent — for marketing emails and optional cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time.

5 Who We Share Data With

  • Stripe — for secure payment processing. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant.
  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider, hosted on secure EU/UK servers.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider.
  • Cooks — we share customer delivery address and order details with the relevant cook to fulfil your order.
  • Law enforcement — if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

All third-party processors are required to handle your data in accordance with UK GDPR.

6 Data Retention

  • Account data — retained for as long as your account is active, plus 2 years after deletion for legal purposes.
  • Order data — retained for 6 years to comply with HMRC financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Marketing consent — retained until you withdraw consent.
  • Technical logs — retained for up to 90 days.

7 Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁️

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

✏️

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

🗑️

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")

⏸️

Right to Restrict

Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

📦

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format

🚫

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@mechchef.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

8 Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We also use analytics cookies to understand how people use our platform.

You can control cookies via your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

9 Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth with row-level security
  • Payment data handled solely by Stripe — we never store card details
  • Regular security reviews of our platform

In the event of a data breach, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and affected users as required by UK GDPR.

10 Contact & Complaints

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at privacy@mechchef.co.uk.

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Helpline: 0303 123 1113
