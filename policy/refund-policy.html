Customer Rights

Refund & Cancellation Policy

We want every MechChef order to be brilliant. If something goes wrong, here's exactly what to expect.

1 Cancellation by Customer

Before cook accepts your order

If you cancel before the cook has accepted your order, you will receive a full refund including the delivery fee.

Within 15 minutes of placing

You may cancel any order within 15 minutes of placing it for a full refund, even if the cook has accepted, provided cooking has not yet begun.

After cooking has started

Once the cook has marked your order as "Cooking", cancellations are at MechChef's discretion. We may offer a partial refund depending on circumstances.

⚠️ If you are not home to receive your delivery and have not notified us in advance, we are unable to issue a refund.

2 Cancellation by Cook

If a cook cancels your order for any reason, you will receive a full refund including the delivery fee within 5–10 business days.

We will also notify you as soon as possible and suggest alternative meals available in your area.

✅ Cook cancellations always result in a full refund. We will never penalise customers for a cook cancelling.

3 Refund Scenarios at a Glance

Scenario Refund Outcome
Customer cancels before cook accepts ✅ Full refund
Customer cancels within 15 mins of placing ✅ Full refund
Cook cancels for any reason ✅ Full refund
Order not delivered due to cook issue ✅ Full refund
Order incorrect or missing items (reported within 24hrs with photos) 🟡 Partial or full refund at discretion
Food quality complaint (reported within 24hrs with photos) 🟡 Partial or full refund at discretion
Customer not home, no prior notice ❌ No refund
Customer cancels after cooking has started ❌ No refund (exceptions at discretion)
Customer dislikes the food (subjective taste preference) ❌ No refund

4 How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of the issue occurring.
  2. Email support@mechchef.co.uk with your order number, description of the issue, and photos where applicable.
  3. We will respond within 2 business days with an outcome.
  4. Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.

⚠️ Refund requests submitted more than 24 hours after delivery without valid reason may not be accepted. Please contact us promptly.

5 Refund Timelines

  • Card payments (Stripe): 5–10 business days to your original card
  • Cash on delivery: Refunds issued via bank transfer within 5 business days

Refund processing time depends on your bank. MechChef has no control over how quickly your bank processes the credit.

6 Your Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under UK consumer law, including the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013. You always have the right to a repair, replacement, or refund for goods or services that are not as described, not fit for purpose, or not of satisfactory quality.

If you are unhappy with our response to a complaint, you may contact Citizens Advice or Trading Standards for further guidance.
