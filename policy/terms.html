  • You must hold a valid Food Hygiene Certificate (Level 2 minimum) from an accredited UK provider.

7 Food Safety & Allergens

MechChef takes food safety seriously. All cooks on our platform are required to:

  • Hold a valid Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate
  • Declare all 14 major allergens on every meal listing (UK Food Information Regulations 2014)
  • Follow good food hygiene practices in line with the Food Safety Act 1990
  • Comply with Natasha's Law (PPDS regulations) for pre-packed food

However, as food is prepared in private home kitchens, MechChef cannot guarantee the absence of allergens or cross-contamination. Customers with severe allergies must exercise caution and contact the cook directly before ordering.
